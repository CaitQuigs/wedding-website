Frequently Asked Questions

Can guests bring their kids?

While we love all of the children in our lives, we have decided to keep our wedding and reception an adults-only event.

How soon should I book my room?

Please book your room as soon as you are sure you are coming to the wedding. We know that everyone says this, but this area is a vacation destination, so the area tends to book up over the winter.

What is the dress code?

We request that you follow a semi-formal wedding dress code. This article has some examples of semi-formal wedding attire.

Can I take pictures or use my phone during the ceremony?

We would like to have an unplugged ceremony, and we would appreciate it if everyone would refrain from taking pictures or using any electronic devices during the ceremony. We have hired professional photographers who will be taking pictures throughout the ceremony, and we will share our photos on this website after the wedding.

Will alcohol be served at the reception?

Yes, we are providing an open bar serving beer, wine and liquor at our reception.

Can you accomodate my dietary requirements at the reception?

We want all of our guests to be comfortable at our reception! We will be providing a vegetarian option for dinner. If you require any other dietary alterations, such as a vegan meal or a gluten free meal, please let us know with your RSVP and we will arrange with the caterers.

What is the COVID-19 policy for this event?

We are monitoring the COVID-19 situation, and will follow the local ordinances regarding COVID-19 at the time of the wedding.

What if I have a question that isn't addressed here?

You can email us for wedding-related questions at caitlinandlyle@gmail.com.
